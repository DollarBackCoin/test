$BACK: The Token for Act II.

Your comeback starts here.

$BACK is the official token of the second act. It was built for the men who have been handed a raw deal. The ones who lost the house. The ones the company showed the door. The ones who looked in the mirror one morning and decided they still had one more round left in them.

This isn't a coin chasing hype cycles. It's a community of builders who understand that real value isn't given. It's reclaimed. Slowly. Deliberately. Together.

The contract itself reflects this philosophy. Loyalty is rewarded. Short-term greed is penalized. $BACK is built for the patient, not the predatory.

$BACK is the quiet vote of confidence from men who have walked the same road.
